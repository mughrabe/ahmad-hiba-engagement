﴿وَمِنْ آيَاتِهِ أَنْ خَلَقَ لَكُم مِّنْ أَنفُسِكُمْ أَزْوَاجًا لِّتَسْكُنُوا إِلَيْهَا وَجَعَلَ بَيْنَكُم مَّوَدَّةً وَرَحْمَةً﴾

By the grace of God, Ahmad Mughrabi and Hiba Shawabkeh, together with their families, warmly invite you to the celebration of their engagement — a night where joy is made whole.
